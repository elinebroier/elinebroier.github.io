Why HBO
I have visiteds many different kind of schools and study programs. At university you get a lot of theory and i would struggle with learning that much theory. That's why the HBO level suits me because I am practical and learn better in practice. I like that at HBO you work result-oriented. The study programme suits my personality because I like to learn new things. I am creative and I like to solve problems. I also like interaction with other people. What appeals to me are the (group)projects you have to do and the feedback you get. You can learn a lot from that feedback and benefit from it. What I also like about ICT is that technology keeps on developing. So there are lot of challenges. There is diversity in ICT jobs. Funny detail: my grandfather on my father's side worked in the ICT world. My dad worked as director of ICT at the HBO in Rotterdam.
