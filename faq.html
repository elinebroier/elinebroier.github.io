HZ Printer: Select the printer to print off in either A3 or in A4 format.
Once the print job has been requested you must register using your HZ pass on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer. Next, select printer option Print / Afdrukken. In the menu, select the multifunctional printer to where the job has been sent. Next, you can see which printer job is ready to be printed. The print job is the completed provided there is sufficient credit on your printing account. When the printing is completed, press Stop on the TouchID, followed by Logout. The print job remains available for up to 24 hours. It is deleted after this period.
